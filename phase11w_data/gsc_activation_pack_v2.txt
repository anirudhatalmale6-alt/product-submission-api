=====================================================================
     WHAT HAPPENS AFTER CONNECTION
=====================================================================

Once either Option A or Option B is complete, here is all the
data that will start flowing into our pipeline for
pethubonline.com:

FROM GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE:

   (1) Indexed Pages
       How many of your pages Google has found and added to
       its search index. This tells us if any pet product
       pages are missing from Google.

   (2) Impressions Per Query
       How many times each search query (like "dog treats
       online" or "best cat food") showed your site in
       Google search results.

   (3) Clicks Per Query
       How many people actually clicked through to your site
       from each search query. This shows which searches
       bring you real visitors.

   (4) Click-Through Rate (CTR) Per Page
       The percentage of people who see your page in search
       results and click on it. Low CTR means we can improve
       your titles and descriptions to attract more clicks.

   (5) Average Position Per Query
       Where your site ranks on average for each search term.
       Position 1-3 is the top of page one, 4-10 is the rest
       of page one, 11+ is page two or beyond.

   (6) Crawl Activity and Errors
       How often Google's bots visit your site and whether
       they encounter any errors (broken links, server
       issues, etc.). We fix these so Google can access all
       your pages smoothly.

   (7) Mobile Usability Issues
       Any problems Google detects when your site is viewed
       on phones and tablets. Since most pet owners browse on
       mobile, this is important for keeping visitors happy.

   (8) Core Web Vitals
       Google's speed and experience scores for your site.
       These measure how fast your pages load, how quickly
       they become interactive, and whether content shifts
       around while loading. Good scores help your rankings.

FROM GOOGLE ANALYTICS (GA4):

   (9) Visitor Traffic
       How many people visit your site, where they come from
       (Google, social media, direct), and which pages they
       view most.

  (10) User Behavior
       How long visitors stay, which products they look at,
       and where they drop off. This helps us understand what
       your customers are interested in.


HOW THIS HELPS PETHUBONLINE.COM:

   All of this data feeds into our SEO pipeline. It helps me:

   -  Find which pet-related searches already bring you traffic
      and double down on those

   -  Discover new search terms pet owners use that your site
      could rank for

   -  Spot and fix any technical issues that hurt your rankings

   -  Track your progress over time so you can see real results

   You will NOT need to do anything else after this one-time
   setup. The data flows automatically and the connection renews
   itself forever.
