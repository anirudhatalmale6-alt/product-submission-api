SAFE-TO-PUBLISH LANE POLICY v1.0
============================================================

SECTION 1: CONTENT THAT QUALIFIES FOR AUTO-PUBLICATION
------------------------------------------------------------

The following content types may be auto-published WITHOUT
manual owner review, provided ALL 12 gates pass:

  - Educational Authority Article
  - Buying Guide Educational Only
  - Faq Expansion
  - How To Guide
  - Comparison Educational Only
  - Safety Guide
  - Care Guide
  - Trust Methodology Page
  - Ai Visibility Improvement


SECTION 2: THE 12 GATES (ALL MUST PASS)
------------------------------------------------------------

  gate_1_educational_only:
    Content contains no affiliate links, no product recommendations requiring evidence, no sponsored content

  gate_2_no_affiliate_links:
    Zero affiliate hrefs detected in content scan (regex: no amazon/shareasale/cj.com/awin links)

  gate_3_no_red_gate_topic:
    Content does not cover pet insurance, regulated health claims, or RED-gated topics

  gate_4_no_regulated_claims:
    No medical claims, no 'vet-backed' without named source, no dosage recommendations

  gate_5_no_fake_signals:
    No 'we tested', 'hands-on testing', 'our veterinarian', 'certified expert', fake rankings, or fake authority signals

  gate_6_metadata_valid:
    SEO title <= 60 characters, meta description <= 160 characters, focus keyword present

  gate_7_schema_safe:
    No Product/Review/AggregateRating/Offer schema. Only FAQ, Article, BreadcrumbList allowed

  gate_8_internal_linking_valid:
    All internal links resolve to existing published pages (no 404s)

  gate_9_trust_wording_safe:
    No exaggerated trust language, no unverified expertise claims

  gate_10_publisher_gate_pass:
    Publisher layer confirms all gates pass with approval_id generated

  gate_11_rollback_available:
    Previous version stored, rollback can be triggered within 72 hours

  gate_12_audit_trail_recorded:
    Full audit record with timestamp, content hash, gate results, and approval_id logged


SECTION 3: CONFIDENCE THRESHOLD
------------------------------------------------------------

  Minimum: 12/12 gates must pass (binary pass/fail)
  Any single gate failure = BLOCKED for manual review
  No partial auto-publish allowed


SECTION 4: CONTENT REQUIRING MANUAL APPROVAL
------------------------------------------------------------

The following ALWAYS require explicit owner approval:

  - Monetized Content With Affiliate Links
  - Product Comparison With Evidence
  - Product Recommendation
  - Content With Product Review Offer Schema
  - Red Gated Topic Pet Insurance
  - Regulated Health Claims
  - Trust Compliance Sensitive Pages
  - Homepage Or Sitewide Changes
  - Bulk Automation Across Live Site
  - Content Failing Any Confidence Gate
